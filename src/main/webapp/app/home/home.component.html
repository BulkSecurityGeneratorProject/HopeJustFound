Mission Statement

To connect those with the greatest needs to those with the greatest assets for the betterment of humanity.

Vision

To provide an opportunity through an on-line platform for all non-profit organizations to list their needs for donated goods, supplies, volunteers and services to those who are willing to fulfill those needs.
